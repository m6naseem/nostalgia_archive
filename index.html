What Nostalgia Means to Me

Nostalgia: a sentimental longing or wistful affection for the past, typically for a period or place with happy personal associations - Oxford Dictionary

Nostalgia is a feeling that takes me back to simpler times. It's the warmth of memories, the little things that shaped who I am today, and the joy of remembering moments that made my childhood special. Whether it's a favorite snack, a TV show, or an inside joke with friends, nostalgia connects me to my past in a meaningful way.
